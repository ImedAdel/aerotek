About Us

Who are we?

AEROTEK is a club founded by ESST Hammam Sousse students, seeking to regroup youngsters who are hooked on Aeronautics, Robotics, and IoT, motivating them to develop their skills, explore their knowledge within a team spirit and create interactive projects based on Hardware/Software aspect.

ESSTHS?

The Higher School of Sciences and Technology was founded on the 6th of June 2006 and it is located in Hammam Sousse. Currently, the school offers courses for 3 fundamental licenses: Maths, Physics, and Computer Sciences, 4 applied licenses, a preparatory cycle, and 6 Masters and 2 PhD's.

What do we do?

ESSTHS supports students' efforts to take the lead in shaping the learning and living environment. Many student organizations work with the Student Activities Office and other university groups to plan events and bring exciting guests, artists, and speakers to the school. Furthermore, we participate in many national and international competitions.

National Robotics Olympiad

National Robotics Olympiad is a competitive event that gives university students the opportunity to work together to create robots and drones that answer definite technical conditions. It will be held on the 10th of February 2019 at the Higher School of Sciences and Technology of Hammam Sousse, Tunisia.
